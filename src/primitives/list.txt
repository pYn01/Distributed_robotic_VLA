A. APPROACH / POSITIONING PRIMITIVES
    1. reach_leaf
    2. position_above_leaf
    3. scan_area

B. DELICATE CONTACT PRIMITIVES
    4. touch_leaf
    5. push_leaf
    6. lift_leaf
    7. hold_leaf

C. COMPLEX MANIPULATION PRIMITIVES
    8. separate_leaves
    9. flip_leaf
    10. move_leaf_sideways

D. INSPECTION PRIMITIVES
    11. inspect_under_leaf
    12. inspect_leaf_surface
    13. inspect_stem

E. RELEASE / RESET PRIMITIVES
    14. release_leaf
    15. return_home



-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. reach_leaf

    Description:
        Move the end-effector close to the target leaf.

    Preconditions:
        A target leaf position is detected.

    Parameters:
        - target_position = (x, y, z)
        - approach_speed

    Effect:
        End-effector reaches proximity of the leaf.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. position_above_leaf

    Description:
        Position the end-effector directly above the leaf without touching it.

    Preconditions:
        Leaf is visible and localized.

    Parameters:
        - offset_height

    Effect:
        Robot hovers above the leaf.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. scan_area

    Description:
        Perform small oscillatory movements to improve visual perception 
        (e.g., exposure, stereo depth, optical flow).

    Preconditions:
        Safe free space around the robot.

    Parameters:
        - scan_radius
        - scan_pattern (circle/zigzag)

    Effect:
        Improved visual information around the leaf.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. touch_leaf

    Description:
        Gently touch the surface of the leaf with minimal force.

    Preconditions:
        End-effector is within 1â€“2 cm.

    Parameters:
        - force_threshold

    Effect:
        Establish controlled physical contact.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. push_leaf

    Description:
        Apply lateral force to move the leaf sideways.

    Preconditions:
        Leaf reachable by the end-effector.

    Parameters:
        - direction (unit vector)
        - displacement

    Effect:
        Leaf is displaced laterally.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. lift_leaf

    Description:
        Slide under the leaf edge and lift it gently.

    Preconditions:
        Leaf edge accessible.

    Parameters:
        - lift_height
        - grip_strength

    Effect:
        Leaf is elevated.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7. hold_leaf

    Description:
        Maintain the leaf in a fixed position.

    Preconditions:
        Leaf is already touched or grasped.

    Parameters:
        - hold_force

    Effect:
        Leaf remains stabilized.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8. separate_leaves

    Description:
        Create a small gap between overlapping leaves.

    Preconditions:
        Two or more leaves detected close together.

    Parameters:
        - spread_distance

    Effect:
        Leaves become separated, enabling visibility.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

9. flip_leaf

    Description:
        Grab a leaf edge and flip it to expose the underside.

    Preconditions:
        Edge reachable.

    Parameters:
        - flip_angle

    Effect:
        Leaf underside becomes visible.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
10. move_leaf_sideways

    Description:
        Move the leaf to the left or right to reveal occluded regions.

    Preconditions:
        Leaf is frontal and reachable.

    Parameters:
        - direction (left/right)
        - distance

    Effect:
        Leaf shifts laterally.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
11. inspect_under_leaf

    Description:
        Move the camera under the leaf to inspect for pests or disease.

    Preconditions:
        Leaf must be lifted or moved.

    Parameters:
        - camera_pose_offset

    Effect:
        Underside is visible.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
12. inspect_leaf_surface

    Description:
        Bring the camera close to the top surface of the leaf.

    Preconditions:
        Leaf surface not occluded.

    Parameters:
        - focus_distance

    Effect:
        Top surface becomes observable in detail.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
13. inspect_stem

    Description:
        Approach the plant stem for close visual inspection.

    Preconditions:
        Stem visible and safe to approach.

    Parameters:
        - camera_offset

    Effect:
        Stem is clearly observed.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
14. release_leaf

    Description:
        Release the leaf if currently held or stabilized.

    Preconditions:
        Leaf is being held.

    Parameters:
        - None

    Effect:
        Leaf returns to natural state.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
15. return_home

    Description:
        Move the robot arm to a predefined safe home pose.

    Preconditions:
        None

    Parameters:
        - home_pose

    Effect:
        Robot returns to safety configuration.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
